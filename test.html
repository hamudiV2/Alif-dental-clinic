Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up?
We recommend visiting every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients with specific dental conditions may need to visit more frequently as advised by their dentist.
Do you treat children?
Yes, we provide dental care for both children and adults. Our team knows how to make kids feel safe and relaxed.
How can I make my teeth whiter?
Professional cleaning and bleaching at the clinic can remove stains. Daily brushing, flossing, and avoiding too much coffee or smoking also help.
How do I take care of my teeth at home?
Brush twice a day, floss daily, eat healthy foods, and limit sugary snacks and drinks.
